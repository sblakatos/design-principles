Overview

Welcome to Visual Media

This site will introduce you to the basics for creating an aesthetically pleasing layout. Each section will break down the basic fundamentals.

There are many rules to keep in mind when creating a layout. These tips will cover the 6 Principles of Design as well as the 4 Laws of the Gestalt Theory.

The examples given can be found in every day, household things.

By the end, you should have a stable idea of what goes into creating a successful layout.
