What is Contrast?

Textbook Definition

"You can employ contrast between filled and empty space. You can employ contrasting sorts of lines or shapes.You may juxtapose contrasting size of objects"(pg 56).

"Introducing a pattern in proximity to no pattern results in contrast. Ditto for texture. Or you may contrast two different kinds of patterns or two different kinds of textures"(pg 56).

"Color and value also offer powerful contrast tools. Using both dark and light values or colors result in contrast"(pg 56).

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Breaking It Down

Contrast can be used when two opposing things are put together. Contrast is an example of emphasis.
