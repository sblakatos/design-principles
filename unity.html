What is Unity?

Textbook Definition

"Unity means that all the parts of the design work together, and everything looks like it belongs together"(pg 60).

"A layout is visually unified if its different parts have visual links or relationships to one another. A good design has some consistency in terms of the pattern type of columns, or rhythm of typography, or style of visuals, etc. Unity refers to oneness, that the result is one cohesive design or visual message"(pg 60).

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Breaking It Down

Your layout could have all the correct working parts, but if they do not link together seamlessly, the message will be lost in clutter. All the different elements of your layout should agree with one another. Achieve this and your message will reach your viewers with the effect you want.
