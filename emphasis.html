What is Emphasis?

Textbook Definition

"The focal point is the most important thing visually on any layout. It is also known as the principle of emphasis"(pg 53).

"The focal point can be anything really, as long as it remains the most eye-catching piece of visual information"(pg 53).

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Breaking It Down

The focal point of a layout is the first thing that a viewer notices. It is the main focus of the page.

It is important to limit each page to only one point of emphasis so your viewer doesn't get distracted.

Real World Example

This is an example of emphasis because this is what draws the eyes attention when you first walk into the room. It is the most colorful and unique thing in the room. Therefore, it should be shown off.
