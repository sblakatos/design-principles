What is Continuity?

Textbook Definition

"Our minds will continue a pattern beyond its ending points. Further, our eyes will follow the direction of a line... Applying this concept can add a sense of direction and movement to your layout"(pg 62).

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Breaking It Down

If you choose to use patterns in your layout, understand that your viewers will follow the flow of the pattern. If your pattern draws the eye left to right, make sure your pattern continues that way. Switching up the flow of a pattern halfway through will confuse and distract your viewers".

Real World Example

The rug in this photograph is a perfect example of continuity. There are many different patterns in the rug but they seem to continue on forever.
